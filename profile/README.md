## What we're building

Captain.Food is a **zero-commission food ordering platform for independent restaurants and food trucks**. Restaurateurs keep their menus, their prices, their customer relationships — and 100 % of every order. Instead of taking a cut, the platform is run as a commons: it is being structured as a **cooperative (ESUS / SCIC)** so the restaurants, couriers, and eaters who use it also govern it.

We're starting small and local: **V0 is launching in Tours, France**, then growing city by city.
